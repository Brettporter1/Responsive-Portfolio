About me

Here's the short and dirty.
My name is Brett Porter, I'm a graphic designer, photographer and soon to be web developer, living in Lenexa, Kansas. I have a passion for creating, whether it be designing and developing the look and feel for a brand, or portraying a feeling by capturing the perfect moment in time. When I'm not working you can usually find me enjoying the outdoors, hanging out with my wife and son, at the gym, or at the local brewery with a flight in front of me.
